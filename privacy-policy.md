## Overview

BowlNote is designed as a private, local-first meal, water, and nutrition tracking app.

The app helps you log meals, track water intake, save reusable meals, calculate nutrition goals, review statistics, export CSV files, and manage optional local reminders.

BowlNote does **not** require an account and does **not** collect, sell, or share your personal data.

---

## Data Stored by BowlNote

BowlNote may store the following information on your device:

- Meal entries
- Water intake entries
- Saved meals
- Nutrition goals
- App settings
- Reminder preferences
- Statistics generated from your own entries

This data is stored locally on your device.

---

## Data Collection

BowlNote does **not** collect your personal data.

BowlNote does not:

- Require user accounts
- Use third-party analytics
- Use advertising
- Use tracking technologies
- Sell or share user data
- Send your meal, water, or nutrition data to external servers
- Sync data with iCloud or any external service in the current version

Your food, water, goal, and statistics data remains on your device unless you choose to export or share it yourself.

---

## Local Notifications

BowlNote may ask for permission to send local notifications.

These may include:

- Water reminders
- Meal reminders
- Weekly review reminders

These notifications are scheduled locally on your device. You can enable or disable reminders at any time in the app settings or in iOS Settings.

---

## CSV Export

BowlNote allows you to export statistics as a CSV file.

CSV export is controlled by you through the iOS share sheet. BowlNote does not automatically upload or send exported files anywhere.

If you choose to save or share an exported CSV file using another app or service, that app or service may handle the file according to its own privacy policy.

---

## Health and Nutrition Disclaimer

BowlNote is intended for personal tracking and informational purposes only.

BowlNote does **not** provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or professional nutrition counseling.

Please consult a qualified healthcare professional before making medical decisions, starting a diet, changing your nutrition plan, or making major lifestyle changes.

---

## Children’s Privacy

BowlNote is not specifically directed to children.

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
